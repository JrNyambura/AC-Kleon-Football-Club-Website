About Us

Welcome to AC Kleon Football Club!

At AC Kleon, we believe in the power of football to transform lives, build character, and foster community spirit. Founded with a vision to nurture young talent from the grassroots level, we are committed to developing the next generation of football stars while making a positive impact in our local communities. Our club offers a safe, encouraging, and fun environment where young players can grow, learn, and excel both on and off the pitch. Through mentorship, professional coaching, and access to competitive leagues, AC Kleon helps players reach their full potential while instilling values such as teamwork, respect, and discipline.

Our Mission:

  • To develop talent from the grassroots level and provide a pathway to professional football.
  • To create a positive and inclusive environment for young players.
  • To give back to the community by engaging in outreach programs and supporting local causes.

Join us in our journey to make football a force for good!
