Nurturing Talent
Building Champions

Are you passionate about football? Do you want to be part of a club that not only nurtures talent but also builds character and community? AC Kleon is the place for you!

We welcome aspiring footballers of all ages and skill levels to join our academy and teams. Whether you dream of playing professionally or simply want to improve your skills, we have a program for you.

How to Join:

  1. Sign Up Online: Fill out the registration form here to get started.
  2. Trial Sessions: New players are invited to attend our trial sessions. We assess talent and potential in a fun and supportive environment.
  3. Meet the Team: Once selected, you’ll be introduced to your coaches, teammates, and the AC Kleon family.
  4. Start Training: Our training sessions are designed to help you grow as a player and as a person. You’ll have access to experienced coaches, state-of-the-art facilities, and a supportive team.

What We Offer:

  • Professional coaching and mentorship
  • Regular matches and competitive leagues.
  • Training in fitness, skills, and strategy.
  • Opportunities to represent AC Kleon in local and regional tournaments.

Join the AC Kleon community today and take the first step in your football journey!
